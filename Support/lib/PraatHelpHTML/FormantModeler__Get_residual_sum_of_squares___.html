FormantModeler: Get residual sum of squares...

Get the residual sum of squares for a formant in the selected FormantModeler.

The residual sum of squares, RSS, is defined as follows

RSS = ∑i=1n (fi - Fi)2,

where fi is the frequency value of the i-ith data point, Fi is the frequency at the i-data point as estimated by the model and n is the number of data points.
