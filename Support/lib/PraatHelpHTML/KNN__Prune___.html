KNN: Prune...

Prune the instance base of the selected classifier.

Settings

Noise pruning degree
A value in the range [0 ... 1] specifying the probability that instances tagged as noisy by the C-Pruner algorithm will be pruned.
Redundancy pruning degree
The probability that instances tagged as redundant by the C-Pruner algorithm will be pruned.
k neighbours
The size of the neighbourhood.
