Sound: To Cepstrogram...

A command that creates a Cepstrogram from every selected Sound.

Settings

Pitch floor (Hz)
determines the effective length of the analysis window: it will be 3 longest periods long, i.e. if the pitch floor is 60 Hz, the window will be 3/60 = 0.05 seconds long.
Time step (s)
Maximum frequency (Hz)
Pre-emphasis from (Hz)
